I do care about small things with strong meanings, and with this philosophy I made the Telegraf NFT project.

You all can participate in the project where everyone is the artist of its own unique pieces. The Telegraf NFT smart contract makes sure that every piece remains forever on the blockchain, on-chain. Be it a message to the love of your life, a memento from an important occasion, a sincere condolence, a greeting for an accomplishment or simply a joke, your creativity is the only limit.

Minting a Telegraf NFT is extremely affordable. With only 1 $MATIC or 1 $FTM plus very low gas fees (in the order of cents of USD) you can have your own NFT, on-chain.

Unleash your creativity and make your own NFT to collect or donate!
